About us

Cimco, Inc. has been serving Arkansas' industrial and utility needs for over 37 years.  We were incorporated in 1975,  mainly doing excavation and industrial maintenance, but we have expanded into utility site construction.

 

Cimco, Inc. can provide customers a single source for all of your projects.  We have a team of professional staff and employees, and a large fleet of equipment to self perform most of our work.

 

Cimco, Inc. is committed to providing quality service, knowledgeable personnel and competitive pricing; and maintaining a safe, healthy work environment for our employees and customers.
